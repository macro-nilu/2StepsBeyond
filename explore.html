About Us

Some of the Best Experiences Begin 2 Steps Beyond Your Comfort Zone.

2StepsBeyond was built from a simple belief that some of the best experiences in life begin when you take two steps beyond your comfort zone. We are a community of passionate trekkers and outdoor enthusiasts brought together by one shared purpose: to create meaningful experiences that inspire people to explore, connect, and grow through adventure.

What started as a small group exploring the Sahyadris has grown into a team dedicated to designing safe, well-planned, and unforgettable outdoor journeys. From treks, camping, and backpacking adventures to experiences that challenge limits and create lifelong memories, every journey we host is built with intention, preparation, and purpose.

△

Growth Through Adventure

We believe adventure is about more than reaching a summit. It is about building confidence, embracing challenges, and discovering what lies beyond your limits.

🛡

Responsible Exploration

We promote sustainable travel, eco-friendly practices, and encourage every explorer to respect, protect, and learn from nature.
